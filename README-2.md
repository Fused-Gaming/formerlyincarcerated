# Bitcoin Land Bond

**Criminal Asset Recovery Initiative - Reentry Housing**

A policy framework and interactive platform for financing reentry housing using seized cryptocurrency and criminal assets.

## Overview

The Bitcoin Land Bond proposes a nonprofit governance model that redeploys seized Bitcoin and digital assets ($15B+ currently in federal custody) to fund dedicated housing infrastructure for formerly incarcerated people.

### Core Thesis

- **$15B** in seized cryptocurrency sits idle in federal vaults
- **600,000** people cycle through the US justice system annually
- **75%** of landlords reject formerly incarcerated applicants
- **20-30%** recidivism reduction with stable housing in first 90 days

## Policy Framework

The complete white paper is available in `/docs/Bitcoin_Land_Bond_White_Paper.docx`.

**Key Components:**
- Land trust model with perpetual deed restrictions
- $500M initial deployment across 5 pilot cities
- 13-member board with reentry leadership representation
- 4-phase implementation roadmap (8+ year path to self-sustaining operations)
- Annual outcome reporting (housing stability, employment, recidivism)

## Governance

**Board Structure:** 13 members across 4 constituencies
- 4 seats: Reentry/Criminal Justice (formerly incarcerated leaders, reentry nonprofits, public defenders)
- 3 seats: Housing & Development (nonprofit developers, community land trusts)
- 3 seats: Finance & Governance (nonprofit CFO, impact investor, legal compliance)
- 3 seats: Community/Government (local officials, neighborhood representatives)

**Community Advisory Board:** 15-20 formerly incarcerated leaders and residents with veto authority over land disposition and major service changes.

## Financial Model

**Capital Allocation ($500M):**
- 45% Land Acquisition & Ground Leases
- 30% Developer Partnerships & Construction
- 20% Resident Services & Outcome Tracking
- 5% Administration & Governance

**Asset Management:**
- 50% of incoming Bitcoin converted immediately to USD (acquisition capital)
- 30% held as Bitcoin (upside capture on appreciation)
- 20% maintained as stablecoins (operational reserves)

## Policy Pathways

### Statutory (Requires Legislation)
- Asset Forfeiture Redirection Act (amend 28 U.S.C. § 524)
- Digital Asset Custody Protocol (executive order)
- Reentry Housing Tax Credit Enhancement

### Executive/Judicial (Immediate Implementation)
- DOJ prosecutorial guidance directing US Attorneys to prioritize Bitcoin Land Bond transfers
- Judicial authorization under 18 U.S.C. § 982 for direct asset transfers
- Philanthropic front-loading ($50-100M) during statutory pathway maturation

## Implementation Timeline

**Phase 1 (Months 1-12): Establishment**
- Nonprofit incorporation & 501(c)(3) approval
- Board assembly
- First $100M Bitcoin transfer
- Pilot city engagement

**Phase 2 (Years 2-3): Initial Deployment**
- 40-50 land parcels acquired
- 20-25 developer partnerships
- 250+ residents housed
- $50-75M in resident services deployed

**Phase 3 (Years 4-7): Scaling**
- Portfolio reaches 80-100 parcels
- $10-15M/year land appreciation
- Expansion to secondary cities
- 1,500+ units; 2,000+ residents

**Phase 4 (Year 8+): Self-Sustaining**
- $15-25M/year from land appreciation
- Operations fully self-funded
- 5,000+ units nationally
- 6,000+ residents served

## Target Outcomes

**Annual Reporting Metrics:**
- Housing Stability: 95%+ 12-month retention
- Employment: 70%+ job placement & retention
- Recidivism: 20-30% reduction vs. control cohorts
- Income Growth: $8K-$12K median earnings increase (Y1-Y2)
- Family Reunification: 60%+ residents with family
- Health Access: 85%+ behavioral health access
